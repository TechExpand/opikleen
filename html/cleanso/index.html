Revitalize Your Space with a Fresh New Look

Optikleen is a professional cleaning company based in Benin City, Edo State, offering full-time and part-time residential and commercial cleaning services. Our comprehensive services include office and janitorial cleaning, residential cleaning, event/pre & post-party cleaning, fumigation and pest control, carpet, rug, and upholstery cleaning, hotel and restaurant cleaning, post-construction cleaning, pre-move/end of tenancy cleaning, facade and window cleaning, as well as marble and floor restoration. For dependable cleaning services in Benin City, contact Optikleen today.
